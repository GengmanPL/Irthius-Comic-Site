Welcome to Irthius!

Irthius is a collaborative fictional world-building project. It began formally in ~2020, and follows the journeys of characters intertwined with the Darkfate Curse. Essentially, Irthius poses the question of:
"What if our game experiences all took place in the same fictional universe?"
